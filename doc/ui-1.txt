#To select an icon from the setting.

You can change the NG icon (namely mine) with an icon selector dialog.
The icon is kept in site about 3 days since you play it.

![icon selector](img/mswp-iphone-iconselector.png)

to display above modal dialog, you tap wrench mark
<i class="fas fa-wrench"></i>
at left upper.

![wrench mark icon](img/mswp-iphone-settings.png)
